About ABC Podcast

Founded in 2018, ABC Podcast began as a passion project between friends who wanted to create meaningful conversations about technology, culture, and innovation. What started as a small side project has grown into a leading podcast network with millions of listeners worldwide.

Our mission is simple: to elevate conversations that matter. We believe in the power of dialogue to inspire change, spark creativity, and connect people across different backgrounds and perspectives. Each episode is carefully crafted to bring you insights from the most interesting minds of our time.

Today, ABC Podcast reaches listeners in over 120 countries and has been featured in major publications like The New York Times, Wired, and The Guardian. But despite our growth, we've stayed true to our roots - authentic conversations that challenge, educate, and entertain.

Our Journey

The first episode of ABC Podcast was recorded in a makeshift studio (a closet with blankets for soundproofing) with just 87 downloads in its first month. Through word of mouth and a commitment to excellence, we grew steadily over the years.

In 2020, we launched our premium membership program, allowing us to invest more in production quality and expand our team. The following year, we introduced our first spin-off show focused specifically on technology and innovation.

Today, we produce three weekly shows with a team of 12 full-time creators and work with sponsors who align with our values. But our most important measure of success remains the same: the thoughtful conversations we spark among our listeners.
